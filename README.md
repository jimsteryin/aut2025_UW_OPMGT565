Final Project for OP565 Business Analytics: Tools for Big Data, Autumn 2025\
Taught by Prof Michael Wagner at the University of Washington Foster School of Business

Using an AirBnB Dataset from Kaggle\
Predicting AirBnB nightly price through Linear Regression\
Generating a Regression Tree for quick price predictions\
Attempting to use K-Means clustering to classify properties into buckets

Applying the above models to a US Real Estate Data snapshot, also from Kaggle, to find profitable AirBnB homes, by zip code\
The mockup will take baseline assumptions, such as interest rate, insurance, occupancy rate, and calculates expected profit/loss
